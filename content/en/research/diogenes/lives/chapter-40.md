<sup>1</sup> Glauco was an Athenian.

He has 9 dialogues contained in 1 volume.

1. The Phidylus
2. The Euripides
3. The Amyntichias
4. The Euthias
5. The Lysithides
6. The Aristophanes
7. The Cephalus
8. The Anaxiphemus
9. The Menexenus

There are 32 others which go under his name, but they are spurious.
